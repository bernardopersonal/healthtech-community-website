Why We Started

We built what we couldn't find.

We are four expats living in Valencia, all working across HealthTech, Pharma, MedTech and Life Sciences. We all have been attending the same generic networking events in the past. The kind where you collect 30 business cards and remember zero conversations.

We wanted to change that and create something different for our industries. A room full of people who actually understand what it takes to bring a medical device to market, run a clinical trial, or build a digital health product from scratch. People who are open to help and collaborate, who are hungry for knowledge and are willing to take action to build the healthcare of the future.

So we stopped waiting and started organizing these events. That's how HealthTech Community Valencia was born. A place where people who care about healthcare innovation stop making small talk and start making real connections and taking real action.

We were tired of showing up to generic meetups and leaving with a stack of business cards. We wanted a room where conversations turn into real connections, collaboration, and business.
