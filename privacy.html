Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 15, 2026

HealthTech Community Valencia ("we", "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data in compliance with the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) 2016/679 and Spain's Ley Organica 3/2018 de Proteccion de Datos Personales y garantia de los derechos digitales (LOPDGDD).

1. Data Controller

The data controller is HealthTech Community Valencia, contactable at:

Email: contact@healthtechcommunity.eu

2. What data we collect

We collect only the personal data you voluntarily provide through our website forms:

  • Newsletter subscription: email address.
  • Speaker/sponsor contact form: full name, email address, company/organization, area of interest, and a short description of your proposal.

3. Purpose and legal basis

We process your data for the following purposes:

  • Newsletter: to send you information about our events and community resources. Legal basis: your consent (Art. 6.1.a GDPR).
  • Contact form: to respond to your inquiry about speaking or sponsoring. Legal basis: your consent and our legitimate interest in organizing community events (Art. 6.1.a and 6.1.f GDPR).

4. How long we keep your data

  • Newsletter subscribers: until you unsubscribe or request deletion.
  • Contact form submissions: for the duration necessary to manage the inquiry, and no longer than 12 months after the last interaction.

5. Who we share your data with

We do not sell your data. Your data may be processed by the following third-party services, solely to operate our forms and communications:

  • FormSubmit.co (form processing)
  • OVH (web hosting, based in France/EU)
  • GitHub (code hosting and deployment)

We ensure all processors comply with GDPR or offer adequate safeguards for international transfers.

6. Your rights

Under GDPR and LOPDGDD, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data.
  • Rectify inaccurate data.
  • Delete your data ("right to be forgotten").
  • Restrict or object to processing.
  • Data portability (receive your data in a structured format).
  • Withdraw consent at any time, without affecting the lawfulness of prior processing.

To exercise any of these rights, email us at contact@healthtechcommunity.eu. We will respond within 30 days.

7. Right to file a complaint

If you believe your rights have been violated, you can file a complaint with the Spanish Data Protection Agency (Agencia Espanola de Proteccion de Datos, AEPD) at www.aepd.es.

8. Cookies

This website does not use cookies or tracking technologies.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

Questions about your data?

Email us at contact@healthtechcommunity.eu and we'll get back to you.
